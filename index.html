Electric Vehicle Optimization

Advancing electric vehicle systems through AI, CFD modeling, and experimental validation to enhance performance, efficiency, and sustainability.

// WHAT IS EVOAU?

EvoAU (Electric Vehicle Optimization @ Auburn University) is a multidisciplinary research initiative focused on advancing electric vehicle technologies through a systems-level approach. The project integrates computational fluid dynamics (CFD), phase change materials, AI-driven optimization, and experimental hardware validation. Our goal is to revolutionize the efficiency, safety, and sustainability of EV battery and energy systems through intelligent simulation and real-world testing.

// OUR MISSION

Our mission is to accelerate the development of advanced electric vehicle systems by transforming how energy management and battery thermal systems are modeled, tested, and optimized. EvoAU leverages advanced CFD simulation, AI-based anomaly detection, and reinforcement learning to create adaptive, intelligent energy management frameworks for real-world EV applications.

Our Teams

Meet the specialized groups driving innovation in the EcoCAR Challenge.

Hardware Team

The Hardware Team focuses on the physical integration and performance of the vehicle’s systems. From high-voltage battery management and drivetrain optimization to embedded systems and power electronics, our engineers ensure that every component works safely and efficiently.

  • Battery and thermal management
  • Powertrain integration and safety systems
  • Vehicle control hardware and sensors
Learn More

CFD Team

The Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) Team leverages advanced simulation tools to study airflow, cooling systems, and aerodynamic performance. By optimizing drag and thermal behavior, they enhance vehicle range, stability, and energy efficiency.

  • Vehicle aerodynamics simulation
  • Cooling and thermal modeling
  • CFD-based design optimization
Learn More

AI & Controls Team

The AI and Controls Team focuses on intelligent vehicle systems — from data-driven decision-making to autonomous driving algorithms. Their work bridges machine learning, control theory, and real-time vehicle feedback to create smarter, more adaptive EV systems.

  • Sensor fusion and data analytics
  • Autonomous navigation and perception
  • Machine learning for system optimization
